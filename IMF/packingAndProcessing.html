Packing And Processing

Step # 1

The very first step is receiving of the raw material from the local markets. This step helps the company to lead to the next level.

Step # 2

In this step, our team members inspect the food carefully. If there are found any unhygienic matters, the seafood is sent back to the supplier immediately.

Step # 3

The next step is that we, as a team, send the fresh, healthy and hygienic raw material to the peeling are for the refinement of the seafood.

Step # 4

The raw material which is headless will now be washed and then chilled in ice-cold water tanks.

Step # 5

After chilling the seafood, it will now be categorized in different variations. The graded food then passes through the process of packing in the boxes.

Step # 6

These boxes are then sent to the freezers for the products to freeze, so that they may stay fresh and do not get rotted. The freezer is set on -40`C and the products are set in the freezer for about 90 minutes.

Step # 7

Since, the boxes are now ready to be sent to the Master Cartons area which is stored in the cold storage. The minimum temperature of the cold storage room is set at -18`C or at -20`C to make the shipment ready for the clients.
