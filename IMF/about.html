Who We Are

IMF established in 2019 specializes in producing quality seafood products for supplying to its local and international clients. We are located in Karachi and are always keen to export the finest quality of fresh, frozen or live seafood to other countries. As an organization of wholesalers of seafood, we aim to bring our production quality and quantity on the top of the list for maintaining a sustainable relationship with our customers. Our delicious seafood is fresh and has numerous varieties of seafood creatures that are scrumptious, fresh and delectable in taste.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide the fresh and handpicked quality seafood to maintain a sustainable customer relationship.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce seafood on reasonably priced rates that everyone may get an experience of having mouthwatering seafood dishes for staying healthy and safe.
